Height Estimation App

A web app that allows you to calculate the height of objects using your phone camera.

Height Estimation App

Previous Next

This app was built as a project in my Engineering Mobile Apps unit at university. The task was to create and app that used the in-built hardware on a mobile phone to calculate the distance to and the height of an object. Using trigonometry, the app calculates the height of and the height to the base of any object. This is done by using your camera to lock in where the base and apex of the object lies, using readings of the accelerometer of your phone, the angle of your phone is calculated and then the height of the object.

Automatica

1st Place Hardhack 2018.

Automatica

Previous Next

This was my team's submission to the 2018 Monash 24-hour Hardhack Competition. We decided to build an automated locker system during the overnight hacking competition. This locker was designed to automate the transfer of goods, when buying and selling items between university students.

As university students we knew all too well the difficulty of organising the buying and selling of textbooks between university students. The main issue we tried to address in our design was the issue of finding a common time and place to meetup and handover the textbook from seller to buyer. This automatic locker system allowed for the seller to drop off the item in their own time (tracked seller based on Student ID card) and then have the buyer pick up the item when they feel like (scan ID card and locker associated with the sale automatically opens).

This project of ours was awarded 1st place at the 2018 Monash Hardhack Competition.

Checkout Chum

A smart trolley streamlining the retail experience with on-the-go product scanning, tracking and one-step payments.

Checkout Chum

Previous Next

Our project makes retail shopping a three step process: arrive, shop and pay - customers sign in to an individually personalised trolley, items are scanned as the customer shops and trolley contents are automatically synced for streamlined payments.

Onboard we have a Raspberry Pi, LCD and RFID scanner facilitating the scanning and live transmission of products the customer has placed in the trolley. These items are tracked in real-time on a cloud database. At checkout time, scanning a loyalty/ID card at the POS system will retrieve the list of trolley items from the cloud and present payment options. Our POS system consisted of a laptop, Arduino and RFID scanner.

Smart Super

1st place prize winner for GovHack 2020.

Smart Super

Previous Next

“The Australian Dream is heading out to the country.”

COVID-19 has presented massive hardships and challenges to many Australians, and in a post-pandemic world, we see a re-imagining of our societies. Financial goals. Lifestyle priorities. Employment opportunities.

Our project gives insights into how we can imagine life in the new normal, using the super withdrawal scheme to give Australians an opportunity to re-evaluate and explore their options for the long-term. We see regional and rural Australia and its thriving local communities as an answer to some of the challenges.

Our platform allows users to see their financial situation at a glance and quantify the impact of living in a regional area, alongside an algorithm that matches their priorities to a suitable area. It could be opportunities for job seekers, the amenities and lifestyle that matches their family’s goals, or how a change of pace could work for them in retirement.

The post COVID-19 Australia is coming soon.

Ready when you are.
